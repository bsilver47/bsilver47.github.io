Welcome to my hidden pages!

If you are reading this, it is probable I did not intend for you to find these pages. The intention is to store links to websites that I will want access to regardless of which computer or platform I am using.

This page, for example, is designed to house a list of links to pages that offer TV and Movies at no cost without signing in (though many offer the ability to sign in just the same).

Please note, I do not have any rights to the contact referenced or linked (short of public access content)

BYUTV

BYUTV offers a wide variety of shows and movies at no cost. As mentioned, BYUTV offers free account creation for saving and syncing progress for what you watch. The website can be accessed at this link. The following are some of the shows that I have enjoyed and am waiting for more episodes, seasons, or am waiting for someone with whom to catch up on watching the show:

  • Secrets at Red Rocks
  • Studio C
  • Relative Race
  • How I Got Here

PlutoTV

PlutoTV offers live TV shows and movies at no cost. As mentioned, PlutoTV offers free account creation for saving and syncing progress for what you watch. The website can be accessed at this link. The following are some of the channels that I like to watch when I have a chance:

  • Doctor Who Classic
  • MythBusters
  • Survivor
  • Tiny House Nation

Tubi

TubiTV, or Tubi, is like a strange mix between BYUTV and PlutoTV in that, like BYUTV, it offers both on demand and live programming, largely or entirely at no cost. As mentioned, Tubi offers free account creation for saving and syncing progress for what you watch. The website can be accessed at this link. While I do not have a list of programs defined as readily as the other sites, the following are some of the channels that I like and/or shows/movies I intend to watch at some point:

  • How To
  • ION Plus
  • Tom and Jerry
  • Monty Python and the Holy Grail

Some fun videos that, aren't really tv but are live streams I enjoy, are:

  • Friends of Big Bear Valley Eagle Cam
  • Yellowstone Geyser Camera
  • The Fish Doorbell (March through June)
